The Conversation Loop

1
User: "Hello!"
→
Claude: "Hi there!"
2
User: "What's 2+2?"
→
Claude: "4"
3
User: "Thanks!"
→
Claude: "You're welcome!"
4
User: "Bye"
→
Claude: "Goodbye!"
What Claude Actually Sees Each Turn ↓
Hello!
↓ grows
Hello! Hi there! What's 2+2?
↓ grows
Hello! Hi there! What's 2+2? 4 Thanks!
↓ grows
Hello! Hi there! What's 2+2? 4 Thanks! Welcome! Bye

Key Insight: Claude has no memory between turns. The entire conversation is re-sent every single time you send a new message. The only reason it "remembers" is because everything gets included in the input.
